Our team is 100% Ukrainian and runs 2 brands in the US, it's Sobaken and Seedra. We work in eCommerce, but now we want to explore a new market and do something good for our country. So we decided to make a donation NFT project and help animals.

Our team is Ukrainians who, like no one else, felt the war on themselves, our main task is to help animals. We show all our costs in a Google spreadsheet. We also provide reporting from shelters.
