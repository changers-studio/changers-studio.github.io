Learning Goals

Walk away having learned more about the fashion industry and what pushes a consumer to buy based on their app and interface rather than the product offered.

A peek at your classes:

Class 1

E-Commerce Business Models

Shop Till You Drop!

Discover What Makes an Online Shop Successful. Discover, research, and study, the dynamics of online shopping and what makes a business successful.

Class 2

Moodboard Design

The Vision and Mission for Your App!

Define your mood board with emotional elements, color palettes, graphic designs, and more!

Class 3

Design Forward Thinking

Make Users Fall in Love with Your App!

You will begin your brainstorming and designing process by analyzing the best apps in e-commerce. Based on success stories like Sephora, Asos, Nike, etc. you will design a user flow experience to make your app fun and easy to navigate for all shoppers.

Class 4

Design Your App Layout

Looks, Matter!

Following the creation of dynamic visuals, and unique graphic elements for your app, you will bring everything together and design a personalized prototype of your first online storefront!
