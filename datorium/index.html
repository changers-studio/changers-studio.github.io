What is the Teacher's Artificial Intelligence Assistant?

The Teacher's AI Assistant is an innovative tool designed to generate learning materials in local languages, based on the world's leading education standards.

In which language will the materials be generated?

Simply select your country and language, and your AI assistant will generate high-quality learning materials in your chosen language. Datorium AI supports numerous local languages.

Is the content created copied from other sources?

No. Datorium AI generates entirely original content. It functions similarly to a human, creating problems, lesson plans, and explanations for students. The Datorium Teacher’s AI Assistant develops and writes educational materials using advanced large-language models powered by machine learning algorithms.

Could there be a copyright infringement?

No. Our platform does not copy content from other sources. It uses Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) to create original content, ensuring there are no copyright infringements. You can freely use this content for teaching purposes.

Does anyone test the Teacher’s AI Assistant?

Yes. Our solution is regularly tested by real teachers from various subjects, who assess the quality of the generated material, its relevance and appropriateness for a school-aged audience, ethics, and many other factors. Additionally, every user can provide feedback, which we take into account when developing the AI assistant.
