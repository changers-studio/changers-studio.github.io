Our story

In March 2007, Orbis Group of Companies embarked on a journey with a simple yet profound mission: to establish a reputable organization grounded in core values of respect, integrity, and commitment.

Beginning as a modest window wall installation crew operating in Vancouver and Calgary, Orbis swiftly gained recognition for upholding its core values, setting the stage for a remarkable evolution.

Attracting esteemed professionals within the building envelope industry, the company's visionary ownership nurtured its growth into the multifaceted corporation it is today.
