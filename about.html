Hello! I am Inna

the founder of Innadove Design.

For me, design is always more than walls, furniture, or colors. It is the stories of people embedded in light, shapes, and textures.

I believe that space has the power to transform — your mood, your feelings, and sometimes even your life.

With over 17 years
in the profession,

I have grown from my very first private projects to establishing my own studio, Innadove Design.

During this time, I earned a diploma in interior and furniture design, became a member of the Union of Designers of Ukraine, chaired the graduation committee at the Department of Interior Design, and taught an author’s course at Stfalkon Academy.

Together with our team we have completed nearly 200 projects in different countries — from Ukraine and Poland to Spain and the USA.

These include residential and commercial interiors, each one becoming a unique story of a space created for people.

Our portfolio features apartments, houses, restaurants, cafés, offices, clinics, hotels, and shops.

For us, every project is not just a “type of space,” but a person with their dreams, lifestyle, and needs.

We believe

Design is not about short-lived trends. It is about solutions that stay with you for years, creating harmony and meaning in your environment.

Where beauty and functionality merge into one. Where the space speaks about you without words.
